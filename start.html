All enemies follow the path. Every enemy has health and gives coind upon death. If not killed, every enemy deals a certain amount of damage when it reaches the end. The main two types of enemies are soldiers and tanks. Soldiers are faster, but esier to kill. They give less coins and take away less health. Tanks on the other hand are slower, harder to kill, but give more coins and take more health away.

Turrets are used to fight the enmies. Every turret has a cost (you cannot place a turret unless you have enough coins). Turrets re placed upon clicking on the field. A turred can only be placed on an empty place (you cannot place a turret on the path, that the enemies are moving on or over bushes or rocks). When you hover over the place, that you want to place the turret on, an indicator will show wheather you can actually place the turret (a red indicator will show that you don't have enough coins or that you cannot place a turret over the place that you are hovering and a lightgrey indicator will show that you can place a turret). Turrets have different fire rates and different bullets that deal more or less damage. Turrets that fire bullets will only target soldiers and turrets that fire rockets will only target tanks.
